Zurl is a gateway that converts between ZeroMQ messages and outbound HTTP requests or WebSocket connections.It gives you powerful access to these protocols from within a message-oriented architecture.The following diagram shows how Zurl may fit in among the entities involved: Any number of workers can contact any number of HTTP or WebSocket servers, and Zurl will perform conversions to/from ZeroMQ messages as necessary.It uses libcurl under the hood.The format of the messages exchanged between workers and Zurl is described by the ZHTTP protocol.
