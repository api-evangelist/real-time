In the last couple months, the blogging platform world has been innovative again.Medium, one of these new players have been leading the way with an amazing interface to write and read content.Very early on, Medium also supported the open web through their addition of RSS feeds.You can consume content posted on Medium in your favorite news reader :) The great news, is that they now support PubSubHubbub as well.Their hub is located at https://medium.
