Previously, we talked about the evolution of push notifications, including how to use Apigee to deliver them in five simple steps.Now, we’d like to specifically discuss Apigee’s role within the push messaging infrastructure.What is the difference between what app services providers do and what Apigee does?App service providers like Apple APNs and Google GCM route messages, while Apigee simplifies the differences between platforms to add more elegant routing.Apigee eliminates the need for you (the developer) to remember universally unique identifiers (UUIDs) for devices, which allows you to target app users or user groups more effectively.
